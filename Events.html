Guide to registration

  • First, pay the registration fee directly into the bank account (Bank account details given below). Then, fill out the registration form to confirm your participation (the link for registration is provided on the respective event page).
  • *For events with no registration fee, participants can directly fill out the registration form to confirm their participation.

Registration Fee includes:

  • Event Kit
  • Only one model exhibition is permitted under one registration
  • Only one paper presentation and publication is permitted under one registration
  • A certificate will be provided for the participation

Registration Fee Payment:

  • The Registration fee can be paid directly to the bank account

After successful fee payment:

  • After you pay the registration fee, fill out and submit the ICI TechBloom Registration form to confirm your participation (link for registration form is provided on the respective event page).
