CreteCraft: Concrete Mix Design Workshop

Principles, Practices, and Applications of Different Types of Concrete
Time: 08:00 AM to 10:00 AM

Venue: Concrete Lab (4011)

For enquiries & assistance:

Dr. Santanu Malik [+91-8257000169]
Register Now
Overview

Concrete Mix Design Workshop is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of one of the most critical aspects of civil engineering – creating high-performance concrete mixes to meet modern construction demands. Concrete mix design is the art and science of selecting the right ingredients—cement, aggregates, water, and admixtures—and determining their optimal proportions to achieve the desired strength, durability, and sustainability. With rapid urbanization, climate change concerns, and increasing infrastructure requirements, innovations in concrete technology have become essential.

This workshop will cover:

  • Fundamentals of Concrete: Understanding materials, properties, and behavior.
  • IS Codal Provisions: Standards and guidelines for mix design and testing.
  • Laboratory Practice: Hands-on training in proportioning and mixing concrete.
  • Types of Concrete: From conventional mixes to advanced concretes like lightweight, high-strength, and sustainable variants.
  • Latest Technologies: Innovations, admixtures, and tools in concrete production.
  • Sustainability and Cost Efficiency: Eco-friendly solutions and economic considerations in design.

Note:

  • Registration for this workshop is free.
  • Participants can directly register by filling out the ICI TechBloom 25 registration form
Outcome
  • Practical Knowledge: Students will learn to design and prepare concrete mixes independently, following codal provisions.
  • Industry Relevance: Exposure to real-world practices, materials, and technologies used in the construction sector.
  • Skill Development: Enhanced laboratory skills, problem-solving, and analytical abilities for structural applications.
  • Sustainability Awareness: Understanding how eco-friendly and cost-effective practices can be integrated into concrete technology.

This workshop bridges the gap between theory and practice, helping students gain confidence and competence in one of the most vital skills for civil engineers.

Expert
Mr. Rishiraj Sharma

The Concrete Mix Design Workshop will be led by an experienced industry professional who brings both technical expertise and practical knowledge to the session. Having served as a Quality Control Engineer at Triangle RMC Plant, the expert has played a pivotal role in ensuring the quality and performance of fresh concrete, managing rigorous testing protocols, and developing optimized mix designs to meet diverse structural requirements.

With in-depth knowledge of IS codes and specifications for concrete, cement, admixtures, aggregates, and testing procedures, the expert has hands-on experience in designing mixes in accordance with Indian Standards, ensuring strength, durability, and sustainability. Their expertise also includes innovations in concrete technology, such as the use of supplementary cementitious materials, high-performance concrete, and eco-friendly practices aimed at reducing environmental impact.
