Overview

A session titled “Career Catalyst Expert Talk - Inspiring Directions, Igniting Success” is being organized for students from different branches and disciplines. The session will bring together a diverse panel of experts, including senior retired government officers, faculty members from reputed coaching institutions, and experienced professionals from various industries.

Through interactive discussions, the experts will share their insights on career opportunities in government, private, and entrepreneurial sectors. They will also guide students on essential skills, future job trends, higher education options, competitive examinations, and industry expectations. The session aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world opportunities, inspiring students to chart a clear and confident career path.

Note:
