Overview
TechnoScript 2025: Student Conference on Engineering Innovations (TS-SCEI 2025)
TechnoScript 2025 (TS-SCEI 2025) invites undergraduate and postgraduate students to submit research papers, technical reports, and case studies on sustainable and resilient engineering innovations.
Papers may align with the following thematic tracks:
  • Track 1: Civil Engineering & Technology
  • Track 2: Architecture and Infrastructure Planning
  • Track 3: Electrical Power Systems and Technology
  • Track 4: Remote Sensing & GIS
  • Track 5: Mechanical Engineering & Technology
  • Track 6: Computer Technology & Applications

Track 1: Civil Engineering & Technology

  • Sustainable & Green Construction
  • Smart Cities & Urban Development
  • Structural Engineering Innovations
  • Transportation Systems & Infrastructure
  • Water Resources & Environmental Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering & Foundation Studies
  • Construction Materials & Technologies

  • Landscape Architecture & Sustainable Housing

  • Geospatial Technologies for Urban Planning

Dear Authors,

We are pleased to invite submissions to TechnoScript 2025 (TS-SCEI 2025), a premier platform for students to showcase innovative research in engineering.

How to Submit

Authors must register for a CMT account. Please refer to:

  • CMT Account Creation Guide
  • CMT Author Submission Form Guide
📌 Submission Procedure
  • Visit the CMT portal: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/TSSCCEI2025
  • Create or log in to your CMT account.
  • Select Create New Submission.
  • Fill in paper details (title, abstract, keywords, authors).
  • Upload the manuscript (PDF, max. 6 pages, double-blind format).
  • Submit before the deadline.
Registration process:
  • After acceptance of the research paper.
  • Participants must first pay the registration fee.
  • After payment, participants must complete the ICI TechBloom 25 Registration Form to confirm their participation.
Publication of Manuscript:

Selected papers will be published in the STM Journals.

Name of the Journals:

  • Recent Trends in Civil Engineering & Technology, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2249–8753.
  • International Journal of Architecture and Infrastructure Planning, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2456-0154
  • International Journal of Electrical Power System and Technology, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2455-7293
  • Journal of Remote Sensing & GIS, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2230-7990
  • Trends in Mechanical Engineering & Technology, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2231-1793
  • Journal of Computer Technology & Applications, a Peer-reviewed journal with E-ISSN: 2229-6964

*Note — Publication is free of cost

Guidelines for Manuscript Submission:
  • Manuscript Submission Checklist
  • Template STM Journals
  • Copyright Form
  • Format: PDF only, A4 size
  • Review: Double-blind review — omit author names and affiliations from the manuscript.

For queries related to the manuscript, please get in touch with icitechbloom25.pce@poornima.org

Guidelines for authors
  • All selected papers will be presented in Hybrid Mode at TS-SCEI 2025.
  • Participation of at least one registered author is mandatory.
  • Language: English
  • Attendance: At least one author must register and present if the paper is accepted.
  • Ethics: Papers must be original, unpublished, and free from plagiarism. Authors need to affirm originality at submission.

The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-reviewing process for this conference. This service was provided for free by Microsoft, and they bore all expenses, including costs for Azure cloud services as well as for software development and support.
