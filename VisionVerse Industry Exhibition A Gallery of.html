About the exhibition

The ICI TechBloom 26 will host a diverse and dynamic exhibition showcasing the latest advancements, technologies, and innovations in civil engineering and allied fields. This exhibition serves as a platform where academia, industry, students, and professionals can interact and exchange knowledge.

Exhibition Highlights:

  • Advanced Construction Materials: Display of innovative, durable, and eco-friendly construction solutions.
  • Adhesives & Reinforcement: Modern bonding agents and reinforcement products for enhanced structural performance.
  • Roofing & Wall Sheets (Asbestos-free): Sustainable alternatives promoting safer construction practices.
  • Modern Paints & Waterproofing: Advanced coatings and protective systems for long-term durability.
  • Tiles & Natural Stones: Aesthetic and sustainable surfacing solutions.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) & Augmented Reality (AR): Immersive technologies for visualization and project planning.
  • Building Information Modelling (BIM): Cutting-edge tools for digital construction and integrated project management.
  • Smart & Sustainable Products: Innovations that reduce environmental impact and support green construction.
  • Precast & Modular Systems: Fast, efficient, and cost-effective construction techniques.
  • Earthquake Resistant Technologies: Solutions for disaster-resilient infrastructure.
  • Waste-to-Resource Innovations: Technologies for recycling and reusing waste in construction.
  • Advanced Surveying Tools (Drones, DGPS, Total Station): State-of-the-art equipment for precise data collection.
  • Books, Study Material & Stationery: Resources to support academic growth and professional learning.
  • Government Projects & Initiatives: Showcasing policies, schemes, and infrastructural advancements.

Outcome of Exhibition
  • Knowledge Sharing: Participants gain exposure to emerging technologies and industry best practices.
  • Industry–Academia Collaboration: Bridges the gap between research and application through meaningful interactions.
  • Innovation & Inspiration: Encourages students and young researchers to think innovatively and adopt sustainable approaches.
  • Networking Opportunities: Provides a platform for stakeholders—industry leaders, academicians, researchers, and students—to connect.
  • Skill Development: Hands-on demonstrations and live displays enhance practical learning.
  • Promotion of Sustainable Practices: Highlights eco-friendly materials and technologies, supporting green infrastructure development.
  • Awareness of Government Initiatives: Informs attendees about current policies, programs, and opportunities in the construction sector.

The exhibition at ICI TechBloom 26 is expected to leave a lasting impact by fostering innovation, promoting sustainability, and building strong connections between academia and industry.
