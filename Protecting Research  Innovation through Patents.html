Protecting Ideas, Powering Progress

Overview

The workshop titled “Protecting Research & Innovation through Patents and Industrial Designs” aims to create awareness among researchers, students, and faculty members about the importance of securing intellectual property (IP) in the form of patents and industrial designs.
It will cover:

  • Fundamentals of patents and design registrations
  • Criteria for patentability and registrability
  • The procedure for filing patents and designs in India
  • How to align research outputs with IP protection

Note:

  • Registration for this workshop is free.
  • Participants can directly register by filling out the ICI TechBloom 26 registration form
Outcome

Through this workshop, participants will learn how to protect their research and innovations through the use of patents and industrial designs. They will learn what qualifies for protection, how to file applications, and why IP is important. The session will also help them identify patentable ideas in their work. The session will empower attendees to become IP-aware innovators, capable of leveraging intellectual property for academic recognition, funding opportunities, and technology transfer.

He holds an M.Tech in Civil Engineering from NIT Agartala and has recently submitted his Ph.D. synopsis at MNIT Jaipur. He is the inventor of 3 granted patents and 5 more filed applications.

Actively engaged in IP awareness initiatives, he regularly conducts seminars and provides professional assistance in patent and design filings. His work is dedicated to bridging the gap between research and innovation through intellectual property protection, with a special focus on supporting startups, students, and academic institutions.
