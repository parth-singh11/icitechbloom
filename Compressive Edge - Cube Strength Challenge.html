ENGGCANVA – Engineering
Beyond Paper


Time: 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM

Venue: Lecture Hall - 2007

For enquiries & assistance:

Dr. Parveen Kumar [+91-7018106242]
Register Now
Overview

Objective:

Students are challenged to design the rotary intersection, ensuring improved traffic flow, safety, and efficiency while accommodating the given PCU values and road widths.

Rules:

  • Teams must select and justify an optimal intersection type (Roundabout or Signalized Intersection).
  • Designs must optimize lane width based on the given PCU and road width.
  • Provisions for pedestrian and bicycle safety must be included
  • Proposals should incorporate road safety measures to reduce congestion and accidents.
  • Teams must suggest sustainable and smart traffic management solutions.
  • The final design must comply with IRC & AASHTO guidelines for capacity analysis.
Guidelines

Teams:

  • 1 or 2 members per team.

Registration process:

  • Participants must first pay the registration fee.
  • After payment, participants must complete the ICI TechBloom 25 Registration Form to confirm their participation.

Design Considerations:

  • Intersection Type: Roundabout vs. Signalized Intersection.
  • Lane Width Optimization: Based on PCU and road width.
  • Pedestrian and Bicycle Safety Features.
  • Road Safety Measures to reduce congestion and accidents.
  • Sustainable & Smart Traffic Management Solutions.

Submission Requirements:

  • Conceptual design drawings (Freehand).
  • Road safety measures and pedestrian crossing plans.
  • Capacity Analysis (Justification using IRC & AASHTO guidelines).
  • Sustainability Proposals (Green infrastructure, smart traffic systems).

Venue Instructions:

  • Teams must report 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • No entry will be allowed 10 minutes after the start of the event.
  • Use of mobile phones is not allowed during the event.
Evaluation and Disqualification Criteria

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Traffic Efficiency Improvement (40%)
  • Road Safety & Pedestrian Measures (20%)
  • Sustainability & Smart Solutions (20%)
  • Innovation & Feasibility (10%)
  • Presentation Quality (10%)

Disqualification Criteria:

  • Receiving external assistance during the event
  • Late arrival after the briefing
  • Refusing to wear mandatory safety gear
  • Unsafe conduct or disturbing other teams

Submission Format: PDF + PPT with detailed explanations, images, and calculations.

Judging Panel: Experts in Transportation Engineering & Urban Planning.
