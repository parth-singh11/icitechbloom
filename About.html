ICI TechBloom 25

Whether you're into cutting-edge tech, hands-on building challenges, creative exhibits, or thought-provoking workshops, this fest has something for everyone. Step into a space where ideas turn into action, and knowledge meets fun. ICI TechBloom 25 is not just a college fest—it is a dynamic platform for young engineers to showcase their skills, explore technological possibilities, and collaborate on ideas that support a sustainable future. The event offers a diverse range of technical activities such as:

  • VisionVerse Industry Exhibitions
  • SkillForge Series Workshops
  • ThinkTide Expert Talks
  • TechnoScript 2025: (TS-SCCEI 2025) (Paper Presentation & Publication)
  • ProtoExpo (Model Exhibition)
  • Engineer's Arena

Participants will engage with academicians, professionals, and industry leaders, gaining exposure to practical knowledge, cutting-edge technologies, and real-world problem-solving. The fest aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and provide opportunities to innovate for real impact. Don't miss your chance to learn, compete, and connect with like-minded peers and industry experts. Join us in building a smarter, greener future — register now and make your mark at ICI TechBloom 25!

Theme: Engineering a Sustainable Tomorrow: Innovate, Integrate, Inspire

ICI TechBloom 25 celebrates the collective spirit of engineering excellence, bringing together students from all branches to shape a smarter, greener, and more resilient future. With a strong foundation in civil engineering and a vision that embraces interdisciplinary innovation, the fest serves as a launchpad for creative ideas, technological breakthroughs, and practical solutions.

From thought-provoking expert lectures and dynamic workshops to cutting-edge exhibitions and competitive technical events, the fest fosters hands-on learning and collaboration. The paper presentation and publication platform opens doors for all engineering disciplines to share impactful research and solutions addressing real-world challenges.

Join us as we blend creativity with construction, tech with transformation, and passion with purpose — because the future we build today is the one we'll live in tomorrow.

Objectives of ICI TechBloom 26

Promote Practical Application & Innovation

Encourage students to apply theoretical knowledge through hands-on activities and competitions, fostering creativity, teamwork, and leadership.

Advance Sustainability Awareness

Inspire budding engineers to explore and adopt sustainable practices and green technologies for a better future.

Enhance Technical Exposure & Collaboration

Provide a platform for interaction with industry professionals and knowledge sharing across diverse engineering disciplines

Poornima College of Engineering

Poornima College of Engineering was established in 2000 with the aim of imparting pragmatic technical education. In its magnificent journey of 25 years, PCE has set various benchmarks and reached at new pinnacles in Engineering Education with dedication, perseverance and devotion. PCE is celebrating its "Silver Jubilee Year" (2024-25) by implementing the academic autonomy conferred by UGC. With student strength of 2500+ students studying in 10 specializations of engineering (CE, AI & DS, CSE (AI), CSE (Cyber Security), CE (R), ECE, EE, ME, Civil & IT), with more than 3.5 Lac square feet of built up area, highly qualified faculties, state of the art infrastructure, good placements and industry-led curriculum, PCE is spearheading its outstanding voyage with motto 'Success is not a destination, it's a journey'.

Jaipur

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is a captivating destination that reflects the grandeur of Rajasthan's royal past and cultural richness. The city is renowned for its magnificent architecture, vibrant art forms, and traditional markets filled with colorful textiles and handicrafts. Jaipur’s heritage lives on through its palaces, ancient structures, and a deep-rooted sense of hospitality. A popular stop for tourists, the city beautifully blends history with modernity, offering a truly immersive cultural experience. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage City, Jaipur also hosts various traditional festivals, showcasing its rich folklore, music, and dance.
